How the Pool Works

1 · Pick 4 Players

Select any 4 professionals from the field. Use the + button on each player tile to add them to your picks.

2 · Designate a Tiebreaker

Mark 1 of your 4 players as your personal tiebreaker. This is used only if another participant picks the exact same 4 players.

3 · Win on Prize Money

The participant whose 4 players earn the most combined prize money wins the pool. Prize is calculated live based on current standings.

Tiebreaker Rules

Different players, same prize total: Lowest Sunday (R4) score among your 4 players wins.

Identical 4-player picks: Designated tiebreaker player's Sunday score decides it.
